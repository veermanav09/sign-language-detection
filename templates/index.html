How to Use

Good Lighting: Ensure your hands are well-lit and clearly visible to the camera.
Hand Position: Keep your hands in the center of the camera view, about 1-2 feet away.
Hold Signs: Hold each sign steady for a few seconds to get accurate recognition.
Audio Output: The system will automatically speak recognized signs. You can also manually trigger speech.
